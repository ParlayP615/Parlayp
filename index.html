flip rules of thumb
→3x minimum on industrial & niche items. Less than that and your time isn't worth it.
→Get the model number. Industrial buyers search exact specs. A nameplate photo = your pricing tool.
→Compare active vs sold listings. 40 active + 8 sold = slow mover. 10 active + 25 sold = buy it.
→Promoted listing eats margin. At 9% on a $200 item that's $18 gone. Price accordingly.
→Industrial can sit 60–90 days. That's fine. The buyer is motivated and pays full price when they come.
→Free grabs priority: Treadmill motors, riding mowers, washing machines, golf carts, hot tub pumps.
→At the bins: Weight = enemy. Casio TV at 4oz beats 20lbs of VHS every time.
